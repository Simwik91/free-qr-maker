Practical Uses for QR Codes

QR codes have countless applications across industries. Here are some popular uses:

Business Cards

Wi-Fi Access

Allow guests to connect to Wi-Fi without sharing passwords

Location Sharing

Provide directions to your business or event location

Document Access

Payment Links
